# AAAI-26 Rules

Status: source-backed stub  
Last checked: 2026-07-15

## Initial Rules

- Use the official AAAI-26 author kit.
- Follow AAAI author policies and presentation requirements.
- AI-generated paper text is prohibited except when the generated text is itself part of experimental analysis.
- Editing or polishing author-written text with tools is allowed under official policy.
