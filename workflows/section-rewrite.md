# Section Rewrite Workflow

## Goal

Improve one section without changing unsupported facts.

## Steps

1. Identify target section and venue.
2. Read paper context and relevant venue checklist.
3. Extract claims, citations, figures, equations, and labels in the section.
4. Apply the matching skill.
5. Return patch suggestions plus risk notes.
6. Ask for approval before applying changes.

## Rules

- Preserve LaTeX commands unless explicitly editing them.
- Do not add citations without provenance.
- Do not strengthen claims unless evidence is present.
