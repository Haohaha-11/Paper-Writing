# Medical Risk Check

Use `skills/limitations-ethics-writer/SKILL.md` and `venues/tmi/rolling/medical-imaging-checks.md`.

## Privacy

- Confirm figures cannot reveal patient identity.
- Remove scanner/site identifiers unless disclosure is approved.
- Check metadata in exported PDFs and supplemental files.

## Validation

- Separate internal validation, external validation, and reader/clinical evaluation.
- Report confidence intervals or uncertainty around key metrics.
- Avoid clinical deployment claims unless supported by the study design.

## Bias And Scope

- Report population, scanner, acquisition, and disease-stage coverage.
- State known exclusions.
- Identify settings where the model may fail.

## Required Author Decisions

- Whether site names can be disclosed.
- Whether the reader study is part of the current submission or future work.
- Whether code/model release is allowed by data agreements.
