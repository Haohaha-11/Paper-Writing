# ECCV 2026 Rules

Status: source-backed stub  
Last checked: 2026-07-15

## Initial Rules

- Paper limit: 14 pages including figures and tables.
- Reference-only pages may extend beyond the main limit.
- Use the official ECCV 2026 paper kit / Springer LNCS style.
- Follow official submission policies for anonymity and formatting.
