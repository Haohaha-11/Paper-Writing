# Rebuttal Workflow

## Goal

Turn reviews into precise responses and revision actions.

## Steps

1. Paste all reviews with reviewer IDs.
2. Cluster comments by issue.
3. Identify high-decision-impact objections.
4. Decide whether each issue needs text clarification, citation, experiment, analysis, or concession.
5. Draft response bullets.
6. Update paper revision plan.

## Output

Use `examples/rebuttal-response-template.md`.
