# IEEE TMI Rolling Rules

Status: source-backed draft  
Last checked: 2026-07-15

## Core Rules

- Regular and special issue initial submissions: 10 pages including references.
- Challenge paper initial submissions: 14 pages including references.
- Initial PDF should be below 40 MB.
- Figures and tables should be placed in the main text.
- Author biographies should not be included in the manuscript.
- Conference extension must be disclosed, cited, and accompanied by the prior conference paper as supporting material.
- Revision/resubmission packages require clean copy, marked-up copy, and response to reviewers.

## Writing Implications

- Medical-imaging claims require clear dataset provenance, validation protocol, and clinical scope.
- Avoid implying clinical deployment unless evidence supports it.
- Distinguish retrospective, prospective, external validation, challenge benchmark, reader study, and ablation-only evidence.
