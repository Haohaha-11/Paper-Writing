# CVPR 2026 Rules

Status: source-backed draft  
Last checked: 2026-07-15

## Core Rules

- Review is double blind.
- Use the official CVPR author kit.
- Avoid author-identifying names, acknowledgments, links, project pages, metadata, and supplement leaks in anonymous submission.
- OpenReview profile and author list policies apply.
- Follow the official CVPR author guidelines for submission and camera-ready stages.

## Writing Implications

- Do not reveal institutions in dataset, code, or acknowledgment text.
- Avoid anonymous-unsafe links in paper or supplement.
- Make figures and captions self-contained because CVPR review is heavily visual.
- Claims about visual quality must be backed by quantitative or user-study evidence when central.
