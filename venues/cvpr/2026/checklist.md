# CVPR 2026 Checklist

- [ ] Uses official CVPR 2026 author kit.
- [ ] Submission is anonymized.
- [ ] No author-identifying acknowledgments.
- [ ] No non-anonymous project/code/data URLs.
- [ ] Figures and tables are readable in the paper.
- [ ] Baselines are recent and venue-appropriate.
- [ ] Ablations support the method claims.
- [ ] Supplement does not reveal identity.
