# NeurIPS 2026 Rules

Status: source-backed stub  
Last checked: 2026-07-15

## Initial Rules

- Main content limit: 9 pages.
- References, appendix, and checklist are outside the content page count.
- Submission PDF size limit noted in official materials: 50 MB.
- Use official NeurIPS 2026 LaTeX style.
- Anonymous submission should not contain author-identifying material or acknowledgments.
