# TMI Medical Imaging Checks

Use these checks for TMI and other medical-imaging manuscripts.

## Evidence

- Dataset source, modality, cohort size, split protocol, and acquisition variation are described.
- External validation is distinguished from internal test performance.
- Reader studies specify readers, protocol, and statistical comparison.
- Challenge papers describe gold standard, evaluation setup, participant permissions, and proceedings link.

## Claims

- Clinical utility claims are scoped to evidence.
- Generalization claims mention scanner, site, demographic, and protocol variation.
- Failure modes and annotation uncertainty are discussed.
- Model comparison includes relevant medical-imaging baselines.

## Ethics And Privacy

- Patient privacy, de-identification, consent, and IRB status are stated where applicable.
- Dataset licenses and access constraints are respected.
